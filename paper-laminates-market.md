<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Paper Laminates</li><li>High Pressure Paper Laminates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper laminate market is divided into two primary types: low pressure and high pressure laminates. Low pressure paper laminates are manufactured using heat and adhesive at lower temperatures, resulting in cost-effective solutions suitable for decorative surfaces and furniture. In contrast, high pressure laminates involve applying higher heat and pressure, producing denser, more durable products ideal for heavy-use surfaces like countertops and flooring. Both types cater to diverse applications in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, emphasizing aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
