<p><p>Synthetic carpets are categorized into several types based on their fiber composition. Nylon carpets are known for their durability and resilience, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Polyester carpets offer vibrant colors and resistance to stains but may compress over time. Triexta carpets combine the benefits of both nylon and polyester, offering softness and strength. Olefin carpets are moisture-resistant and suitable for indoor and outdoor use. Other types include various blends and specialty fibers, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
