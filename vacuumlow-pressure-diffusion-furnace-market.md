<p><p>The vacuum/low pressure diffusion furnace market comprises two primary types: horizontal and vertical furnaces. Horizontal furnaces feature a layout where substrates are positioned parallel to the ground, allowing for uniform temperature distribution and easy loading, making them suitable for batch processes. Conversely, vertical furnaces operate with substrates placed upright, optimizing space and enabling efficient heat transfer. Both types cater to industries like semiconductor manufacturing and materials processing, offering advantages in various diffusion applications based on production needs and spatial limitations.</p></p>
