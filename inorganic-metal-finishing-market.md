<p><p>The Inorganic Metal Finishing Market encompasses various processes that enhance metal surfaces for corrosion resistance, aesthetic appeal, and improved performance. Anodizing involves creating a protective oxide layer on aluminum. Cladding bonds a layer of material to enhance durability. Conversion coatings chemically alter the surface for better adhesion and corrosion protection. Electroplating deposits a metal layer onto a substrate using an electric current, while electroless plating employs a chemical reaction without electricity. Other methods include passivation and surface treatments for specialized applications.</p></p>
