<p><p>The industrial drones market can be categorized into two primary types: rotary-wing drones and fixed-wing drones. </p><p>Rotary-wing drones, often known as quadcopters, are highly maneuverable and ideal for tasks requiring vertical takeoff and landing, like surveying and monitoring infrastructure. They are typically used for close-range applications due to their ability to hover.</p><p>Fixed-wing drones feature a traditional aircraft design, offering greater flight endurance and covering larger areas efficiently. They excel in mapping, agricultural monitoring, and long-range inspections, making them suitable for extensive landscape assessments.</p></p>
