<p><p>The metal casing market encompasses various production methods such as stamping, die casting, extrusion, and CNC machining. Stamping involves pressing metal sheets into specific shapes, ideal for high-volume production with precision. Die casting utilizes molten metal to fill molds, providing complex shapes with a smooth finish. Extrusion forces heated metal through a die to create continuous profiles, suitable for components like tubes. CNC machining uses computer-controlled tools for high-precision cutting and shaping, allowing for detailed designs and customization.</p></p>
