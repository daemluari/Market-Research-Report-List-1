<p><p>The water filter housing market comprises several key types, including Reverse Osmosis (RO), Ozone Water Purifiers, Activated Carbon systems, and Distillers. RO systems use a membrane to remove impurities, ensuring high purity. Ozone purifiers utilize ozone gas for disinfection, effectively eliminating bacteria and viruses. Activated Carbon filters absorb contaminants through carbon granules, enhancing taste and odor. Distillers boil water to remove contaminants, effectively purifying it. Each type caters to specific purification needs, contributing distinctly to safe drinking water solutions.</p></p>
