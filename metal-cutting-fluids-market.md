<p><p>The metal cutting fluids market consists of four primary types: </p><p>1. **Emulsion Metal Cutting Fluids**: Mixtures of oil and water, providing cooling and lubrication.</p><p>2. **Semi-Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids**: A blend of oils and water-soluble additives, offering enhanced cooling and lubrication properties.</p><p>3. **Synthesis Metal Cutting Fluids**: Formulated from synthetic ingredients, these fluids provide superior performance with reduced environmental impact.</p><p>4. **Neat Oil Metal Cutting Fluids**: Pure oils used in operations requiring high lubrication without water, ideal for specific machining processes.</p></p>
