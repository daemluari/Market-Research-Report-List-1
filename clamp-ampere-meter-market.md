<p><p>Clamp ampere meters are essential tools for measuring current in electrical systems without breaking the circuit. The market features two main types: transformer-type and electromagnetic system clamp meters. Transformer-type clamp meters utilize a current transformer to measure AC current, providing high accuracy and a wide measurement range. In contrast, electromagnetic system clamp meters rely on electromagnetic induction to determine current flow, offering simpler designs and cost-effectiveness. Both types serve various industrial and commercial applications, ensuring safe and efficient electrical monitoring.</p></p>
