<p><p>The primary battery recycling market consists of two main types: wet and dry recycling. Wet primary battery recycling involves the use of aqueous solutions and chemical processes to extract valuable materials, often resulting in higher recovery rates of metals. In contrast, dry primary battery recycling utilizes mechanical and thermal processes, focusing on the separation of components without chemical treatment, making it more environmentally friendly. Both methods aim to reduce waste and recover resources from used primary batteries efficiently.</p></p>
