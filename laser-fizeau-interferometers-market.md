<p><strong>The Laser Fizeau Interferometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homodyne</li><li>Heterodyne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser Fizeau interferometer market comprises two main types: homodyne and heterodyne interferometers. Homodyne interferometers utilize a single frequency laser, providing precise measurements of optical path differences by analyzing phase shifts in the interference pattern. In contrast, heterodyne interferometers combine two different frequencies, enabling enhanced measurement sensitivity and speed by detecting frequency variations in the interference signals. Both types are vital in applications such as optical testing, metrology, and surface profiling, catering to diverse industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
