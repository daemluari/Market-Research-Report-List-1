<p><strong>The Aerial Refueling Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hose-Type Aerial Refueling</li><li>Hard-Tube Aerial Refueling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerial refueling services market encompasses two primary types: hose-type and hard-tube aerial refueling. Hose-type refueling utilizes flexible hoses that allow for greater maneuverability during in-flight refueling, commonly seen in naval operations. In contrast, hard-tube aerial refueling employs rigid tubes, ensuring a precise connection between the aircraft during refueling, which enhances stability but limits movement. Both methods cater to military and commercial needs, improving operational range and mission flexibility for various aircraft types.</p></p>
