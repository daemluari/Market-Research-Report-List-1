<p><ul><li>Paramagnetic</li><li>Ferromagnetism</li><li>Superparamagnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Contrast Agents market is categorized into three main types: paramagnetic, ferromagnetic, and superparamagnetic. Paramagnetic agents enhance imaging by shortening relaxation times, making them suitable for visualizing tissues and vascular structures. Ferromagnetic agents are less commonly used due to safety concerns but can create significant magnetic fields for specific applications. Superparamagnetic agents offer improved imaging contrast due to their variable magnetic properties, providing high sensitivity and resolution in detecting minute anomalies in tissues and organs.</p></p>
