<p><p>The imaging diagnostic catheters market encompasses various types, each serving specific medical applications. Angiography catheters enable visualization of blood vessels through dye injection during X-ray imaging. Ultrasound catheters facilitate real-time imaging using sound waves, aiding in various procedures. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) catheters provide high-resolution imaging for examining tissue microstructures. Electrophysiology (EP) catheters are used to assess electrical pathways in the heart. Other market categories may include specialized catheters for unique imaging techniques or procedures, enhancing diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
