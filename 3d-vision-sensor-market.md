<p><p>The 3D vision sensor market encompasses various technologies, notably stereo vision and structured light. Stereo vision captures depth information by using multiple cameras to analyze disparities between images, allowing for accurate 3D reconstruction. In contrast, structured light projects a known pattern onto the scene, with the deformation of this pattern helping to gauge depth and surface contours. Both methods are crucial for applications in robotics, automation, and augmented reality, offering unique advantages in accuracy and adaptability for different environments.</p></p>
