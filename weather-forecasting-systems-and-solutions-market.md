<p><p>Weather forecasting systems and solutions can be categorized into three primary types: satellite-based systems, ground-based systems, and airborne systems. Satellite-based systems leverage orbital satellites to monitor atmospheric conditions and provide global weather data. Ground-based systems use a network of weather stations and radar systems to gather local data for accurate short-term forecasts. Airborne systems involve aircraft equipped with meteorological instruments, enabling real-time data collection at various altitudes, enhancing weather predictions and operational planning for aviation and other sectors.</p></p>
