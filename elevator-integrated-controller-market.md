<p><strong>The Elevator Integrated Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Automatic Operation</li><li>Selective Collective Operation</li><li>Group Automatic Operation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Elevator Integrated Controller market consists of various operational types that enhance elevator efficiency. Single Automatic Operation allows a lone elevator to serve calls independently. Selective Collective Operation enables multiple elevators to respond to calls based on predefined logic, improving service time. Group Automatic Operation manages a cluster of elevators, optimizing traffic flow by distributing calls among them. Each type enhances user experience and system efficiency, catering to different building requirements and passenger volumes.</p></p>
