<p><ul><li>Embedded Hardware</li><li>Embedded Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive embedded market encompasses two main types: embedded hardware and embedded software. Embedded hardware includes microcontrollers, sensors, and communication devices that facilitate vehicle functionality and connectivity. This hardware is essential for systems like advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and infotainment. Embedded software, on the other hand, comprises the code and algorithms that control the hardware, enabling features such as vehicle control, diagnostics, and user interfaces. Together, they drive innovation and enhance vehicle performance, safety, and user experience.</p></p>
