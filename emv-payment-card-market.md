<p><p>The EMV payment card market encompasses three main types: contact cards, contactless cards, and dual interface cards. Contact cards require physical insertion into a terminal for transactions. Contactless cards utilize RFID technology, enabling quick payments by simply tapping the card near a reader. Dual interface cards combine both features, allowing users to choose between inserting the card or tapping it, enhancing convenience and speed in transactions. This diversity caters to varying consumer preferences and evolving payment technologies.</p></p>
