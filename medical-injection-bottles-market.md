<p><p>The medical injection bottles market is categorized into two main types: mold-made bottles and tube-made bottles. Mold-made bottles are produced using injection molding techniques, allowing for precise shapes and sizes tailored for specific applications. In contrast, tube-made bottles are created from pre-formed tubes, which can then be shaped or sealed, offering flexibility in design. Both types are essential for storing and delivering injectable medications, ensuring safety, sterility, and ease of use in medical settings.</p></p>
