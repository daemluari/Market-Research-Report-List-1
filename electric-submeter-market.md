<p><ul><li>Current Transformer</li><li>Rogowski Coil</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric submeter market is categorized into three main types: Current Transformers (CT), Rogowski Coils, and other technologies. Current Transformers are widely used for measuring alternating current by producing a proportional current in their secondary winding, suitable for high voltage applications. Rogowski Coils offer a flexible, lightweight option for measuring AC currents, providing accuracy and ease of installation. Other market types may include digital and smart submeters, which integrate advanced features for real-time monitoring and data analysis.</p></p>
