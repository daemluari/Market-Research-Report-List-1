In contrast, Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems leverage technologies such as Selective Laser Melting and Electron Beam Melting to create strong, complex metal parts. Both markets are rapidly growing due to advancements in technology, increasing applications in aerospace, automotive, and healthcare, driven by the demand for customization and efficiency.</p></p>
