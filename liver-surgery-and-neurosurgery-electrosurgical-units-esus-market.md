<p><p>The liver surgery and neurosurgery electrosurgical units (ESUs) market consists of various types, including monopole electrosurgical units, bipolar electrosurgical units, and vessel sealing units. Monopole ESUs utilize a single active electrode to cut or coagulate tissue, making them versatile for various procedures. Bipolar ESUs employ two electrodes in close proximity, minimizing thermal damage to surrounding tissues, ideal for delicate surgeries. Vessel sealing units specialize in sealing blood vessels, ensuring hemostasis while reducing surgical complications, enhancing patient safety and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
