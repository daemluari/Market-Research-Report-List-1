<p><p>Poppet dampers are control devices used in various applications, classified into two main market types: two-way and three-way. A two-way poppet damper allows airflow in and out, enabling simple control of air direction, ideal for basic systems. In contrast, a three-way poppet damper can redirect airflow to multiple paths, facilitating more complex ventilation and climate control systems. This versatility makes three-way dampers suitable for larger operations requiring precise airflow management, while two-way dampers serve more straightforward environments.</p></p>
