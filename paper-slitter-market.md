<p><p>The paper slitter market is categorized into three types: manual, semi-auto, and full-auto. Manual slitters require operators to perform most tasks, making them suitable for low-volume production with high precision. Semi-auto slitters enhance efficiency by automating certain functions, allowing for quicker setups and increased output while still requiring human intervention. Full-auto slitters offer advanced automation, enabling high-speed operations with minimal manual input, making them ideal for large-scale production and maximizing productivity in industrial settings.</p></p>
