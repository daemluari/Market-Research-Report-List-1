<p><strong>The Belt Cleaning Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scraper</li><li>Brush</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt cleaning equipment is essential for maintaining the efficiency and hygiene of conveyor systems. There are primarily two types: scrapers and brushes. Scrapers are designed to remove bulk material and debris from the belt surface, ensuring minimal carryback and preventing contamination. Brushes, on the other hand, provide a gentler cleaning method that effectively removes fine particles and dust, enhancing the belt's operational performance. Both types play a vital role in prolonging belt life and maintaining overall system efficiency.</p></p>
