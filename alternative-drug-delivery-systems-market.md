<p><p>Alternative drug delivery systems encompass various methods designed to enhance the efficacy and convenience of medication administration. Nasal delivery allows rapid absorption through the nasal mucosa, suitable for quick systemic effects. Transdermal systems use patches to deliver drugs through the skin, ensuring sustained release and bypassing the digestive system. Transmucosal delivery targets mucosal membranes in the mouth or rectum for fast absorption. Other systems may include inhalation or ocular methods, providing innovative options to improve patient adherence and therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
