<p><p>The ULM (Ultra-Light Motorized) aircraft market comprises three main types: piston engine, electric motor, and turboprop. Piston engine aircraft are fueled by gasoline and are known for their simplicity and efficiency in short-distance flights. Electric motor aircraft utilize batteries for propulsion, offering quieter operation and reduced environmental impact, ideal for urban air mobility. Turboprop aircraft combine turbine engines with propellers, providing high speed and better performance at higher altitudes, suitable for regional travel and cargo transport.</p></p>
