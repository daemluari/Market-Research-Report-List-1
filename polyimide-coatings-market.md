<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide coatings are typically divided into two main types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset polyimides are characterized by their irreversible curing process, resulting in a robust, heat-resistant material ideal for high-temperature applications. In contrast, thermoplastic polyimides can be re-melted and reshaped upon heating, offering flexibility and easier processing. Both types serve various industries, including aerospace, electronics, and automotive, due to their excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties, making them suitable for demanding environments.</p></p>
