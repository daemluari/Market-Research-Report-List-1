<p><p>Spherical roller bearings are essential components in various applications, and their market types are defined by the materials used for cages. Sheet steel cages, either stamped or laser-cut, offer durability and cost-efficiency. Polyamide cages, made through injection molding, provide lightweight and corrosion-resistant options. Brass cages, available as stamped or machined versions, combine strength with a traditional finish. Machined steel cages offer superior precision and robustness, catering to high-performance requirements, making each type suitable for specific operational needs.</p></p>
