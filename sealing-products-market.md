<p><p>The sealing products market encompasses various types essential for leak prevention and system integrity. General gaskets and gasket sheets are versatile sealing materials used in diverse applications. Metallic and semi-metallic gaskets provide high strength for extreme conditions, while elastomer packings, like O-rings, offer flexibility and resilience in sealing. Fluoropolymer components, such as PTFE and PFA, are known for their chemical resistance and durability. Finally, the packing market includes sealing materials designed for dynamic applications, ensuring reliable containment in machinery.</p></p>
