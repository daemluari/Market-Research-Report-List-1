<p><p>The PET Clamshell market encompasses various packaging styles, including mock clamshells, two-piece clamshells, and tri clamshells. Mock clamshells feature a single-piece design that offers easy opening and closing, often used for lightweight products. Two-piece clamshells consist of a separate base and lid, providing enhanced protection and visibility for contents, making them ideal for retail displays. Tri clamshells have three sections, allowing for compartmentalization; these are commonly utilized for multi-item packaging and food items, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
