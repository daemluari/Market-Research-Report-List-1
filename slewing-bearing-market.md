<p><strong>The Slewing Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Gear Type Slewing Bearings</li><li>Internal Gear Type Slewing Bearings</li><li>Non-gear Type Slewing Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slewing bearing market encompasses three main types: External Gear Type, Internal Gear Type, and Non-Gear Type slewing bearings. External Gear Type features gears on the outer ring, allowing easy integration with external drive systems, making them ideal for cranes and excavators. Internal Gear Type has gears on the inner ring, providing compact designs, suitable for applications like mobile machinery. Non-Gear Type slewing bearings lack gear teeth, offering simplicity and weight savings, often used in fixed installations and lightweight structures.</p></p>
