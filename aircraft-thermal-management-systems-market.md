<p><strong>The Aircraft Thermal Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cycle</li><li>Vapor Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Thermal Management Systems market is primarily categorized into Air Cycle and Vapor Cycle systems. Air Cycle systems utilize ambient air to regulate cabin and equipment temperatures, relying on the properties of air expansion and compression. In contrast, Vapor Cycle systems operate by circulating a refrigerant that absorbs and dissipates heat, ensuring efficient temperature control. Both systems are crucial for maintaining optimal thermal conditions in aircraft, enhancing comfort, safety, and performance during flights.</p></p>
