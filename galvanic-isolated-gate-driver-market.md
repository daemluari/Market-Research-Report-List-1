<p><p>The galvanic isolated gate driver market consists of isolated IGBT gate drivers and isolated MOSFET gate drivers. Isolated IGBT gate drivers are designed to facilitate the control of insulated gate bipolar transistors, ensuring safe operation in high-voltage applications. In contrast, isolated MOSFET gate drivers serve similar functions for metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors. Both types utilize isolation techniques to protect signal integrity and enhance safety in power electronics, making them essential for automotive, industrial, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
