<p><p>The He-Ne laser projection heads market encompasses various types, including Single Line, Crossline, Multi-Line, and Dot Matrix. Single Line projectors produce a solitary laser line for basic alignment tasks. Crossline projectors create intersecting lines for precise positioning in construction and layout work. Multi-Line projectors generate multiple laser lines for comprehensive coverage in larger areas. Dot Matrix projectors, on the other hand, create a grid of laser dots, useful for detailed reference points in complex installations. Each type serves specific applications across various industries.</p></p>
