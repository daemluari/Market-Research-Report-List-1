<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Leak Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Reflection</li><li>Penetration</li><li>Resonance</li><li>TOFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic leak testers operate on various market types, including Pulse Reflection, Penetration, Resonance, and Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD). Pulse Reflection measures ultrasonic waves reflected back from leaks, while Penetration detects gas or fluid leaks through materials. Resonance utilizes frequency response to identify leaks by resonating within a structure, and TOFD employs the travel time of sound waves to analyze defects. Each method offers unique advantages for detecting leaks in diverse applications, enhancing reliability and safety.</p></p>
