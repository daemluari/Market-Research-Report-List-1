<p><p>The Power Lithium Battery Recycling Market can be categorized into three main types: recycling, reuse, and cascade utilization. Recycling involves breaking down batteries to recover valuable materials like lithium, cobalt, and nickel for new battery production. Reuse focuses on extending the life of batteries by repurposing them for secondary applications, such as energy storage systems. Cascade utilization refers to using batteries for lower-demand applications after their primary use, thus maximizing their lifespan and minimizing waste while still offering value.</p></p>
