<p><p>The Infrared Dust Sensor market is segmented into two main types based on output: UART (Universal Asynchronous Receiver-Transmitter) Output and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) Output. UART Output sensors provide digital signals for dust concentration measurements, making them suitable for applications requiring serial communication. On the other hand, PWM Output sensors emit varying pulse widths corresponding to dust levels, allowing for easy integration with microcontrollers. Both types are essential for air quality monitoring in industrial, commercial, and environmental applications.</p></p>
