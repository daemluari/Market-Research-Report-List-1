<p><ul><li>Active Packaging</li><li>Intelligent Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart packaging encompasses two main types: active packaging and intelligent packaging. Active packaging interacts with the environment to extend shelf life and enhance food preservation, often through moisture control, oxygen scavenging, or antimicrobial agents. In contrast, intelligent packaging features sensors and indicators that provide real-time information about the condition of the product, such as freshness or temperature changes. Both types aim to improve consumer safety, product quality, and overall supply chain efficiency by offering enhanced functionalities beyond traditional packaging.</p></p>
