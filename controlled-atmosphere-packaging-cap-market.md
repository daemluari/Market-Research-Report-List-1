<p><p>Controlled Atmosphere Packaging (CAP) is an advanced packaging technology that modifies the composition of the internal atmosphere of packages to extend the shelf life of products, particularly perishable goods like fruits, vegetables, and meats. By reducing oxygen levels and increasing carbon dioxide or nitrogen, CAP slows down oxidative processes, microbial growth, and respiration rates of packaged products.</p><p>The Controlled Atmosphere Packaging (CAP) market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for fresh food products and a rise in global consumption of packaged foods. The market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period, reflecting heightened consumer awareness of food safety and quality. The integration of smart packaging technologies is also on the rise, enabling real-time monitoring of packaged goods.
