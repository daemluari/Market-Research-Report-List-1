<p><p>The XBC battery market comprises two main segments: P-Type and N-Type silicon wafers. P-Type silicon wafers are formed by doping silicon with elements that create “holes” for electron movement, making them suitable for various applications due to their cost-effectiveness. In contrast, N-Type silicon wafers are doped to provide excess electrons, offering higher efficiency and better performance in high-temperature conditions. These distinct types cater to different technological needs and performance criteria within the battery landscape, driving innovation and market expansion.</p></p>
