<p><p>The tube extrusion line market is divided into two primary types: single-layer and multi-layer systems. Single-layer tube extrusion involves producing a uniform tube from a single material, ideal for applications requiring simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multi-layer tube extrusion incorporates multiple materials to enhance functionality, providing benefits such as barrier properties, improved durability, and customized properties for specific applications. Multi-layer tubes are often sought after in packaging and industrial applications for their versatility and enhanced performance.</p></p>
