<p><p>The heavy iron casting market encompasses three main types: grey iron casting, ductile iron casting, and malleable iron casting. Grey iron casting is known for its excellent machinability and high wear resistance, making it ideal for engine blocks and pipes. Ductile iron casting, characterized by its enhanced tensile strength and ductility, is used in automotive and heavy equipment applications. Malleable iron casting, offering good shock resistance and malleability, is typically utilized in agricultural and plumbing components, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
