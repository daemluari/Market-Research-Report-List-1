<p><p>Automotive Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) is an advanced emissions control technology designed to reduce nitrogen oxides (NOx) in exhaust gases from diesel engines. By injecting a urea-based solution, known as diesel exhaust fluid (DEF), into the exhaust stream, SCR systems facilitate a chemical reaction that converts harmful NOx into harmless nitrogen and water vapor. This technology is crucial for meeting stringent emissions regulations set by governments around the world.</p><p>The Automotive Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period.
