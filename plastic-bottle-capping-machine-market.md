<p><p>The plastic bottle capping machine market is categorized into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic machines operate independently, maximizing efficiency and minimizing human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input but automate significant portions of the capping process, offering a balance between efficiency and labor. Manual machines rely entirely on human operation, suitable for low-volume production, providing flexibility and lower initial investment costs, but with higher labor intensity.</p></p>
