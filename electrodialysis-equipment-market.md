<p><p>The electrodialysis equipment market comprises two primary types: continuous and batch electrodialysis. Continuous electrodialysis systems operate in a constant flow mode, allowing for ongoing separation and purification of ions, making them ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, batch electrodialysis processes treat fixed volumes of solutions in discrete cycles, offering flexibility for smaller operations or varying product requirements. Both types serve essential roles in industries such as water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, focusing on efficient ion removal and recovery.</p></p>
